On my iBook G3 900 w/ Mac OS X 10.4.5, muttng will never allow the
system to sleep unless compiled w/ --enable-buffy-size. Compiled with
the latter, mailboxes that previously received new mail are listed as
having new mail even though this is not true.

In comparison, mutt 1.5.11 vvv-patch shows the mailbox behavior
described above when compiled WITHOUT --enable-buffy-size, and there
are no sleep problems at all.

-Andre
